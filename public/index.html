Frequently Asked

Questions, answered

How does the 14-day free trial work?
Sign up with your email and company name — no credit card needed. You get full access to the Business plan for 14 days. At the end of the trial, you can pick a plan and pay, or your account becomes read-only until you do. No surprise charges.
Is my data really separate from other customers?
Yes. Each customer (tenant) has their own isolated workspace inside our database. You cannot see, search, or affect another customer's data — the system enforces tenant isolation at the database layer for every single request.
Can I export my data if I leave?
Always. From AccountsCore, you can export your full chart of accounts, journal entries, trial balance, and all financial statements as Excel or CSV at any time. Your data belongs to you.
Do I need to be in the Maldives to use YenuERP?
No, but it's designed for Maldivian businesses (MVR-native, MIRA-compliant). If you're outside the Maldives but want a flexible multi-currency ERP, it still works — just turn off MIRA reporting in Settings.
How is payment handled?
You can pay monthly or annually via bank transfer (BML, MIB), card, or bulk invoice for annual plans. We send a proper invoice with GST applied, MIRA-compliant.
What if I need help setting up?
All paid plans include onboarding help. Business and Enterprise plans get a dedicated onboarding session. Starter plan gets a guided setup wizard and email support. You're never on your own.
